## Additional Information for Reviewers

1. **Content Nature**: 
   - App is about orchid plant care and gardening
   - All content is educational and hobby-related
   - No violent, sexual, or inappropriate content

2. **Community Guidelines** (to be added in app):
   - No offensive language
   - No spam or advertising
   - Only orchid and plant-related content
   - Respect other users
   - No personal attacks

3. **Moderation Process**:
   - Users can report posts via Firebase
   - Admin reviews reports within 24-48 hours
   - Violating content removed immediately
   - Repeat offenders may be banned

4. **Privacy Protection**:
   - No private messages (reduces risk)
   - No location sharing
   - Minimal personal info displayed
   - Users control what they share
